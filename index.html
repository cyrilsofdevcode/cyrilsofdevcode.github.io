About Me

Hi, I’m a passionate Software Developer, skilled Graphics Designer, and an emerging AI Engineering enthusiast with a creative mindset and a strong technical foundation. I specialize in building clean, efficient, and user-friendly applications using modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Python. From front-end interfaces to back-end systems, I enjoy developing full-stack solutions that are both functional and elegant. In addition to software development, I have a strong background in graphic design. I create compelling visual content — from logos and branding to UI/UX elements — that communicates effectively and enhances user experience. My journey into AI and machine learning is driven by curiosity and a desire to innovate. I’ve worked on beginner-to-intermediate level AI projects using Python libraries like TensorFlow, Keras, and scikit-learn, exploring areas like data analysis, prediction models, and intelligent automation. I’m always learning and evolving — combining creativity with code to build meaningful digital experiences. Whether it's developing an app, designing a brand, or exploring AI solutions, I bring passion, precision, and originality to everything I do. Let’s create something extraordinary.
